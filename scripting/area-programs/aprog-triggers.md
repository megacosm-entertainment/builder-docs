```
AreaProgram Triggers:
random              reset
```

| trigger | phrase |
|:--------|:-------|
| random | percent (0-100) |
| reset | percent (0-100) |

**Random** triggers will use their phrase as a percent chance to fire each tick.

**Reset** triggers will use their phrase as a percent chance to fire each area reset.
