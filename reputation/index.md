# Reputation

Reputation factions (reputations) are builder-created relationship systems that track how a player stands with a specific group, faction, or organization. Players gain or lose reputation points through quests, kills, and scripted events.

Each reputation has a set of **ranks** representing levels of standing. As a player accumulates points they move through ranks, which can unlock dialogue options, shop discounts, quest access, and more.
